About

My name is Kyle and I am a Junior at Irvington High School. Using my expertise in Software Development, 3D Printing, and CAD Modelling, I love to empower other in the community. My passion mainly lies in helping underserved and underpriveledged groups, namely Special Education students.

My Story

Starting in 2019, I came to the realization that Special Education Students lacked the opportunities to pursue STEM and Robotics.Besides this, there was a strong stigma against Special Needs Students as well.

With my experience from being on a FIRST Tech Challenge robotics team, we realized that by using our technical skills, we would be able to not only help provide opportunities but also pave the way for a more accepting and connected community for general and special education people alike.

This became my drive.

From this moment on I realized that learning was an extension of being able to help others. The more I learn the more I am able to teach and empower help others in the community.
